Subject please read  important information for participants in the enron
savings plan
due to current business circumstances  on november     enron  s board of directors adopted an amendment to the enron corp  savings plan  the amendment includes the following changes  which became effective on november  
 savings plan contributions that are deducted from your november  paycheck will be matched by enron in cash rather than enron stock  the cash match will be placed into the fidelity freedom  fund  the default fund for the plan 
 after november   enron will no longer match your savings plan contributions  as an exception  the company will continue the union matching contributions to the plan 
 the savings plan has always permitted employees to place their contributions in enron stock or one or more alternative investment options  effective immediately  diversification within the plan will also be allowed  regardless of your age  from company contribution accounts  this removes the restriction for all employees under the age of  to diversify the company contributions held in their account  these accounts include all prior company contributions  qnec  and company match accounts 
to move funds 
on the web  choose  savings plan   restricted employer acct transfer  reallocation  from the drop down menu 
on voice response  choose  request for fund transfer  reallocation of your restricted employer accounts  
this will allow you to move money from the four formerly restricted accounts  company matching  union matching  prior matching  and qnec
