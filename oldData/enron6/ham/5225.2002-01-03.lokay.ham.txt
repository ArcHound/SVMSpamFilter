Subject dynegydirect  new trade floor functionality
effective monday  january     dynegydirect will institute a  new look 
on our trade floors  the new look will have active and inactive markets
designated with green and red lights to the right of the product line 
upon opening your individual trade floor  window the window may not contain
the usual number of products  you have two choices to make 
choice  
a  show all mkts  button will be on the upper right of the screen  if you
check this  all the markets you have saved in your trading window  s  will
appear  just as they always have  whether the markets are active or
inactive  you will not see the green and red lights to the right of the
product line 
choice  
if you do not check the  show all mkts  button  the window may be blank
until dynegy begins posting prices and volumes and a  refresh  button
appears on the top center of the window  clicking on the  refresh  button
will enable active markets  which will be indicated by a green light to the
right of the product line  the light will remain green for  seconds after
activation 
the  refresh  button will reappear as additional markets become active or
as an existing market becomes inactive  when dynegy places a product on
hold  inactive market  the price and volume will disappear and a red light
will appear to the right of the product line after  minutes of
inactivity 
clicking on the  refresh  button will add all active markets and  or remove
all inactive markets 
additional note 
any new trading windows you create from monday forward will default to
choice
