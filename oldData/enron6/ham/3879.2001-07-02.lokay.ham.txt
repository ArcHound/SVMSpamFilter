in bp energy  s red rock contract    bp has certain termination rights if tw defaults on certain obligations  so that everyone is aware of the rights and obligations of both parties  they are stated below 
transwestern must certify to bp by december    or such date as the parties may agree upon  that transwestern has received acceptable licenses  permits  except air permits   approvals  right  of  way interests and utilities for construction and operation of the red rock expansion  if this condition is not met  or otherwise waived by the parties  bp shall have the right upon thirty    days prior written notice  exercised no later than sixty    days after december    to terminate the agreement 
transwestern must certify to bp by march    that transwestern has received acceptable air permits  if this condition is not met  or otherwise waived by the parties  bp shall have the right upon thirty    days prior written notice  exercised no later than sixty    days after march    to terminate the agreement
